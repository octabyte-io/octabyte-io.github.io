## Overview

HortusFox is a powerful self-hosted plant management and tracking system designed for plant lovers who want complete control over their gardening activities. With advanced plant tracking, collaborative management, and integrated task scheduling, HortusFox simplifies plant care for individuals and groups alike. Users can assign plants to specific locations, manage attributes like tags and notes, and collaborate with family, friends, or partners through secure user authentication.

HortusFox goes beyond simple plant tracking by offering inventory management for gardening supplies, a built-in task system for scheduling plant care, and additional utilities like a calendar, weather forecasts, and a plant identification tool.
